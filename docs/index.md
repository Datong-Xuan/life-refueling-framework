## Canonical definition

**Life Refueling** is a lifestyle and cultural framework for observing depletion and supporting the restoration of vitality, clarity, rhythm, relationship, and meaning through intentional everyday practices, environmental contact, and social connection.

**生命充能**是一种用于观察持续耗散，并通过日常行为、环境接触与社会连接，支持身体活力、心理清明、关系支持、生活节律与意义感恢复的生活方式与文化实践框架。

## Important boundary

Life Refueling is not medical treatment, psychological diagnosis, a clinical assessment tool, or a claim about physical, quantum, or supernatural energy. It does not replace qualified professional care.
