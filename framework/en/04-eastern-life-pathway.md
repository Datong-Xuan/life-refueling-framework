# Eastern Life Pathway

Datong Xuan expresses Life Refueling through:

## Gather

Return from dispersion to the body, present moment, and inner center.

## Nourish

Provide conditions for life to flourish through nature, food, rhythm, relationship, and care.

## Resonate

Recover sensitivity to oneself, others, culture, and the living world.

## Return to Wholeness

Not perfection, but the reappearance of inherent completeness.

## Sustain

Allow returning to become an everyday rhythm.
