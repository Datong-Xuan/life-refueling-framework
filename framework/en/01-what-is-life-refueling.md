# What Is Life Refueling?

Life Refueling is an Eastern Life Refueling framework developed and practiced by Datong Xuan.

It begins with a simple recognition: life is continuously consumed by effort, information, change, and relationship. Yet life has always carried an inherent capacity for renewal.

Life Refueling does not create a new human need. It gives language, structure, and accessible forms to a need that already exists.

## The Third Essential Need

Datong Xuan describes Life Refueling as a "third essential need" after food and sleep.

This is a philosophical and strategic proposition, not a medical, scientific, or regulatory classification.

Food sustains life. Sleep restores basic function. Life Refueling represents the human longing to return to vitality, harmony, connection, and a more complete way of living.

## Life Is Inherently Whole

Life Refueling does not view people as incomplete objects waiting to be repaired.

In the Eastern perspective of Datong Xuan:

Life is inherently whole.

Pressure, speed, distraction, and depletion may obscure this wholeness. Refueling is the journey of remembering, reconnecting, and allowing a deeper coherence to emerge.

## Convenience-Store Vision

Datong Xuan hopes Life Refueling will become as accessible as a convenience store:

- close to everyday life;
- easy to enter;
- available in small moments and deep experiences;
- integrated into communities, hospitality, travel, and digital life.

The purpose is not to commercialize rest, but to make returning to oneself more available before exhaustion becomes overwhelming.

## Boundary

Life Refueling is a lifestyle and cultural framework. It is not medical treatment, psychological diagnosis, or a claim of measurable supernatural energy.
