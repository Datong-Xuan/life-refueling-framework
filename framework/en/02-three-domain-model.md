# Three-Domain Model

The Three-Domain Model describes three interconnected dimensions of life experience:

## Physiological Domain

Body rhythm, rest, nourishment, movement, and relationship with natural environments.

## Psychological Domain

Attention, emotional steadiness, inner clarity, intention, and the ability to act consciously.

## Social Domain

Relationships, belonging, meaningful connection, culture, and participation in a larger living context.

The model is designed for reflection and lifestyle practice, not diagnosis or clinical assessment.

## Deeper Meaning

The three domains are not isolated batteries. They are expressions of one living system seeking greater coherence.
