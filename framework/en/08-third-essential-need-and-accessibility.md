# The Third Essential Need and Accessibility

Life Refueling identifies an overlooked human need: the need to recover vitality and connection before exhaustion.

Datong Xuan's long-term vision is to make Life Refueling as accessible as a convenience store.

Not a luxury retreat only, but everyday access:

- neighborhood spaces;
- hospitality experiences;
- digital guidance;
- simple daily practices.

Life already needs refueling. Datong Xuan develops the language and forms that allow this existing need to become visible.
