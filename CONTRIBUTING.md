# Contributing

Contributions should improve clarity, evidence discipline, bilingual consistency, accessibility, or machine readability.

## Requirements

- Preserve the canonical term **Life Refueling**.
- Distinguish philosophy, lifestyle practice, research proposal, and established evidence.
- Do not add medical, psychological, supernatural, or guaranteed-effect claims.
- Provide reliable sources for scientific or regulatory statements.
- Mark exploratory content explicitly.
- Keep English and Chinese definitions semantically aligned.
- Do not imply endorsement by Datong Xuan through unauthorized use of trademarks or logos.
