# Use Cases｜应用场景

## Honey and food rituals

Honey may function as:

- food;
- an optional Quick Recharge or Daily Nourishment ritual;
- a symbol of gathering, transformation, storage, and sharing in bee ecology.

It must not be described as medicine, a frequency product, or proof of therapeutic effect.

## Tea, incense, and quiet practice

Tea, Traditional Chinese Incense Arts, reflection, and quiet time may support Stillness Recharge by reducing input and creating a pause.

They are cultural and lifestyle practices, not medical or psychological treatment.

## Life Refueling Retreat

A Life Refueling Retreat is a low-interruption hospitality experience combining accommodation, space, nature, sleep opportunity, food rituals, tea, incense, optional reflection, and personal autonomy.

It is not a clinic. Participation in activities should be optional, and no fixed outcome should be promised.

## Xiaoyuan AI

Xiaoyuan AI may:

- explain Life Refueling;
- suggest one low-burden entry point;
- interpret non-clinical check-in results;
- provide general lifestyle information;
- help users reflect and plan.

Xiaoyuan must not diagnose, prescribe, simulate professional therapy, encourage emotional dependency, claim spiritual authority, or handle emergencies as a substitute for local services.
